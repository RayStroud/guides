How to Read Upgrade Tables

  • Header # - defense rating of the armor in the corresponding upgrade level
  • Bonus - for an individual piece of armor
  • Set Bonus - wearing full set of all three pieces that are upgraded twice
  • Special - additional ability (may not be displayed on the armor screen)
  • [Dragon] - from which Dragon to source the parts for the corresponding armor piece (Head, Body, or Legs)
  • Location - where to find the armor piece

Effects

Identical effects from different sources do not stack past the maximum level limit.
