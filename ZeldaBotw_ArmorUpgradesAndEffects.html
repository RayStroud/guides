How to Read Armor Tables

  • Header # - defense rating of the armor in the corresponding upgrade level; below are the ingredients required to upgrade to that level
  • Effect - effect when wearing an individual piece of armor
  • Set Bonus - effect when wearing a full set of all three pieces that have been upgraded twice
  • Special - additional effect (may not be displayed on the armor screen)
  • [Enemy] - from which Enemy to source the parts for the corresponding armor piece (Head, Body, or Legs)
  • Location - where to find the armor piece
  • Buy - coming later - price to buy the armor piece, if sold in a shop (uses Â¹ and Â² if more than one shop)
  • Sell - coming later - price to sell the armor piece (increases with upgrade level)
