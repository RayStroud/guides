Armor

How to Read Armor Tables

  • Header # - defense rating of the armor in the corresponding upgrade level; below are the ingredients required to upgrade to that level
  • Effect - effect when wearing an individual piece of armor; effects marked with ✴️  are special and may not be displayed on armor screen
  • Set Bonus - effect when wearing a full set of all three pieces that have been upgraded twice
  • Location - where to find the armor piece
  • [Enemy] - from which Enemy to source the parts to upgrade the corresponding armor piece (Head, Body, or Legs)
  • Buy - potentially coming later - price to buy the armor piece, if sold in a shop (uses ¹ and ² if more than one shop)
  • Sell - potentially coming later - price to sell the armor piece (increases with upgrade level)

Effects

Identical effects from different sources do not stack past the maximum level limit.

Cold Resistance

Effect Source Description
Heat • Fire
• Fire Weapon		 • increases the temperature to survive in cold areas
(equivalent to 1 Level ❄️  Cold Resistance)
• +10 fire elemental damage
• instantly kills ice enemies
• sets enemies fleeing on fire
❄️  Cold Resistance
(2 Levels)		 • Ruby Circlet
• Snowquill Armor Pieces
• Vah Medoh Divine Helm
• Spicy Food, Elixirs		 • withstand cold in snow regions, high altitudes, or desert nights
(adjusts the temperature range that damages)
❄️  Unfreezable • Snowquill Set Bonus
• Zant's Helmet		 • eliminates elemental ice damage (e.g. ice enemies and ice weapons)
• prevents being trapped in ice
• does not protect from extreme cold water in snow regions

Heat Resistance

Effect Source Description
Cold • Ice Weapon • decreases the temperature to survive in hot regions
(equivalent to 1 Level ☀️  Heat Resistance)
• +10 ice elemental damage
• instantly kills fire enemies
• traps enemies in ice, making the next hit 300% damage
(enemies in ice react to electricity as if in water)
☀️  Heat Resistance
(2 Levels)		 • Sapphire Circlet
• Desert Voe Armor Pieces
• Island Lobster Shirt
• Frozen Food
• Chilly Food, Elixirs		 • withstand heat in the desert sun
(adjusts the temperature range that damages)
☀️  Heat-Resistant • Gerudo Set Bonus • Level 1 ☀️  Heat Resistance
🔥️  Flame Guard • Flamebreaker Armor Pieces
• Vah Rudania Divine Helm
• Fireproof Elixirs		 • survive in the fire-inducing extreme heat of Death Mountain
🔥️  Fireproof • Flamebreaker Set Bonus • eliminates elemental fire damage
(e.g. fire, fire enemies, fire weapons, Death Mountain geysers)
• prevents catching on fire
• does not protect from lava
